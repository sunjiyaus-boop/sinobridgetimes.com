양념자 기자 · 2026.06.12

本報記者 楊念慈 · 2026.06.12

據MBC，火警於上午10時30分許發生，灑水裝置啟動，十餘分鐘內撲滅。

Fire at SK hynix Cheongju Plant; 11 Hospitalized after Gas Leak

A fire at SK hynix's Cheongju plant was extinguished in about ten minutes by sprinklers, but a small leak of process fluorine gas sent 11 people to hospital.
By Yang Nyeom-ja · 2026.06.12

Per MBC, the fire began around 10:30am and was quickly put out.

광고 문의 · 300×250

The leaked gas measured about 5ppm—tiny but highly toxic—with 11 reporting eye irritation and other symptoms.

The company and fire authorities are probing the cause and equipment damage.

Production-line safety bears on industrial competitiveness; follow-up measures are expected after the investigation.
