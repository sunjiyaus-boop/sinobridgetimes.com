정치 소통의 명암

쇼츠는 정치인이 대중과 가까이 소통하는 효과적 수단이 될 수 있다. 그러나 복잡한 사안을 단순화하고 자극적 장면에 의존하면, 정책 논의의 깊이를 해칠 수 있다는 우려가 따른다.

전문가들은 정치 콘텐츠가 화제성에 치우칠수록 정작 중요한 정책과 책임의 영역이 가려질 수 있다고 본다. 소통 방식의 변화가 정치의 본질을 흐리지 않도록 균형이 필요하다는 것이다.

華橋時報는 매체 환경의 변화가 정치 소통의 형식을 바꾸는 것은 자연스럽지만, 그 내용의 진정성과 책임은 변하지 않아야 한다고 본다. 형식의 화려함이 본질을 대체할 수는 없다.

Korean politics hooked on 'shorts': a ruling-party lawmaker's 'jab at Trump' video from a UN meeting stirs controversy

Communication centered on short videos ('shorts') has spread into politics, stirring controversy. A ruling-party lawmaker posting a video to the effect of 'landing a jab on Trump' during a UN meeting came under scrutiny.
By Jo Gye-hyeon · 2026.06.21

As the 'shorts' style of delivering messages through brief, provocative clips takes deep root in politics, voices worrying about its side effects are growing. Even solemn diplomatic stages becoming material for video content is drawing controversy.

광고 문의 · 300×250

A ruling-party lawmaker who produced and posted a video in the vein of 'landing a jab on Trump' while attending a UN meeting became a target of criticism—accused of consuming an international diplomatic venue as self-promotional content.

The light and shadow of political communication

Shorts can be an effective means for politicians to communicate closely with the public. But concern follows that simplifying complex matters and relying on provocative scenes can damage the depth of policy debate.

Experts hold that the more political content leans toward buzz, the more the truly important domains of policy and responsibility can be obscured. Balance is needed so that a change in the form of communication does not blur the essence of politics.

Hwagyo Times holds that while it is natural for a changing media environment to change the form of political communication, the sincerity and responsibility of its content must not change. Flash of form cannot replace substance.
