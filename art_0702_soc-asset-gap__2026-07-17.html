20대 자산 격차 34배… 부동산 등 실물자산은 206배

한국 20대의 자산 상위 20%와 하위 20% 간 격차가 34배까지 벌어진 것으로 나타났다. 부동산 등 실물자산 격차는 206배에 달했다.
이문창 기자 · 2026.07.17

출발선의 격차가 커지는 것은 부모 자산의 이전, 즉 '부의 대물림' 영향이 크다는 분석이 나온다.

A 34-fold wealth gap among Koreans in their 20s—206-fold in real assets

The gap between the top and bottom quintiles of Koreans in their twenties has widened to 34 times in total assets, and 206 times in real assets such as property.
By Lee Mun-chang · 2026.07.17

Analysts attribute the widening starting line largely to parental transfers—inherited wealth.

광고 문의 · 300×250

Asset polarization within the young generation cascades into gaps in marriage, childbirth and housing plans.

Experts urge stronger asset-building programs for youth and restoration of the housing ladder.

The current mix of a weak won and high prices hits asset-less young people hardest.

Young people in the Chinese community are no exception, making community-level sharing of startup and housing information—and mutual aid—ever more important.
