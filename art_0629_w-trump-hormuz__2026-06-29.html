여년재 기자 · 2026.06.29

트럼프 대통령은 이란이 호르무즈 봉쇄를 거론하자 합의가 성사되지 않을 경우 이 핵심 해상로를 직접 관리하겠다고 압박했다고 CNN이 전했다. 호르무즈는 세계 원유의 상당량이 지나는 길목이다.

本報記者 呂年財 · 2026.06.29

川普於伊朗提及封鎖荷莫茲後施壓稱，若協議未成將直接管理此核心海運要道，CNN報導。荷莫茲為全球大量原油必經之地。

Trump threatens to 'take over' Hormuz; markets watch the cost of tanker transit

President Donald Trump threatened to 'take over' the Strait of Hormuz if no deal is reached with Iran. Markets watch the real cost tankers pay, not the announcement that transits have resumed.
By Yeo Nyeon-jae · 2026.06.29

After Iran raised the prospect of closing Hormuz, Trump pressed that the U.S. would manage the key sea lane directly if a deal falls through, CNN reported. A large share of the world's crude passes through the strait.

광고 문의 · 300×250

Iran cited closure in response to Israeli strikes in Lebanon, while the U.S. paired military and diplomatic pressure to keep the strait open to international shipping.

Market participants weigh 'at what insurance and risk cost do tankers pass' more than any announcement that transits have resumed. Rising costs feed instantly into crude prices.

Sinophone Bridge Times reads the situation through actual transit data and cost shifts rather than the rhetoric of threats. Hormuz's stability is proven by passage, not declaration.
