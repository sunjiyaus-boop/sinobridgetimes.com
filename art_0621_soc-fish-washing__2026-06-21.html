상식과 과학의 간극

오랫동안 당연하게 여겨 온 조리 습관이 과학적 근거와 어긋나는 경우가 있다. 세척이 청결을 보장한다는 통념과 달리, 일부 식재료는 씻는 과정에서 오히려 오염이 확산될 수 있다.

전문가들은 식재료별로 적절한 손질·조리 방법을 아는 것이 식중독 예방의 첫걸음이라고 본다. 충분한 가열, 도마·칼의 분리 사용 등 기본 수칙이 중요하다는 것이다.

本報記者 楊念慈 · 2026.06.21

魚於烹調前清洗一向被視為理所當然的常識，惟一家食安機關對此提出意外見解、引發話題：一律清洗未必是良策。

광고 문의 · 300×250

據該機關說明，以流水沖洗魚或肉類時，水花四濺恐使表面細菌散布廚房各處，即所謂「交叉污染」風險。充分加熱烹調對殺菌更有效。

常識與科學的落差

長久被視為理所當然的烹調習慣，有時與科學依據相悖。與清洗保證潔淨的通念不同，部分食材於清洗過程中反而可能擴散污染。

專家認為，了解各食材適切的處理與烹調方法，是預防食物中毒的第一步，充分加熱、砧板與刀具分開使用等基本守則至為重要。

華橋時報指出，日常習慣亦值得以正確資訊檢視。家庭餐桌的安全，始於小小常識的更新。

'Of course you wash fish before cooking?'—a food-safety agency's surprising answer

Washing fish before cooking has been taken for granted, but a food-safety agency offered a surprising answer: rinsing before cooking can actually work against hygiene.
By Yang Nyeom-ja · 2026.06.21

Rinsing fish before cooking has long been considered obvious common sense, but a food-safety agency drew attention with an unexpected view: washing unconditionally is not always best.

광고 문의 · 300×250

According to the agency, when fish or meat is rinsed under running water, splashing can spread surface bacteria across the kitchen—the risk of so-called 'cross-contamination.' Thorough cooking with heat is more effective for killing germs.

The gap between common sense and science

Cooking habits long taken for granted sometimes clash with scientific evidence. Contrary to the notion that washing guarantees cleanliness, some ingredients can actually spread contamination in the process.

Experts say knowing the proper preparation and cooking method for each ingredient is the first step in preventing food poisoning, with basics such as thorough heating and separating cutting boards and knives mattering.

Hwagyo Times notes that even everyday habits are worth checking against accurate information. Safety at the family table starts with a small update to common sense.
