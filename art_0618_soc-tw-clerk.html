전금문 기자 · 2026.06.18

자유시보는 타이베이지검이 사건 증거로 보관 중이던 성적 영상을 몰래 촬영하고 이를 공유한 혐의로 소속 서기관을 기소했다고 보도했다. 검찰이 내부 구성원의 비위에 대해서도 예외 없이 사법 처리에 나섰다는 점에서 주목된다.

Taipei Prosecutors Tough on Their Own: Clerk Who Secretly Filmed and Shared Evidence Video Indicted

The Taipei District Prosecutors Office indicted one of its own clerks for secretly filming and sharing a sexual video held as case evidence, the Liberty Times reported—an example of strict handling without shielding insiders.
By Jeon Geum-mun · 2026.06.18

The Liberty Times reported that the Taipei District Prosecutors Office indicted an in-house clerk on suspicion of secretly filming and sharing a sexual video it held as case evidence. It drew notice that prosecutors pursued judicial action without exception even for an internal member's misconduct.

광고 문의 · 300×250

Case evidence—especially sensitive material containing a victim's privacy—demands strict management and confidentiality. Privately filming and distributing it is a grave offense that inflicts secondary harm on the victim.

Trust in judicial and investigative agencies flows from a rigorous capacity for self-cleansing of internal misconduct. Holding even 'one's own' accountable without exception is key to preserving an institution's fairness and trust.

This newspaper reports judicial matters on a factual basis while also stressing the perspective of victim protection. Strict management of evidence is fundamental to protecting victims' rights.
