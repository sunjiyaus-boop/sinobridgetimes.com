청소년 보호 흐름의 확산

UAE Becomes First in Arab World to Ban Under-15 Social Media Use

By Hyeong Deok-chang · 2026.06.19

Kyunghyang reported the UAE will be the first in the Arab world to ban under-15 social media use, a Middle Eastern entry into a global trend of regulating minors' access.

A spreading youth-protection trend

Several countries have recently moved to set age limits on minors' social media use, driven by concerns over effects on study, sleep and mental health, and exposure to harmful content.

The UAE measure will likely place age-verification duties on platforms. Ensuring effectiveness while balancing free expression and privacy remains a challenge in implementation.
