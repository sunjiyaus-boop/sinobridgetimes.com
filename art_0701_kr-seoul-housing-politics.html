증시는 사상 최고 행진을 이어가지만 체감 경기와 주거 불안은 그대로다. 이 괴리가 서울시장 선거의 핵심 변수로 떠올랐다.
손기요 기자 · 2026.07.01

조선일보에 따르면 자산 시장 호황과 ‘내 삶은 나아지지 않았다’는 박탈감이 충돌하며 부동산 민심이 선거판을 흔들고 있다.

광고 문의 · 300×250

'Stocks rise, so why not my life'—property sentiment shakes the Seoul mayoral race

Equities keep setting records, but felt conditions and housing insecurity have not budged. That gap has emerged as a key variable in the Seoul mayoral election.
By Son Gi-yo · 2026.07.01

A boom in asset markets colliding with a sense that 'my life hasn't improved' is shaking the race, Chosun Ilbo reported.

광고 문의 · 300×250

With home and jeonse costs, loan curbs and supply jitters overlapping, voters rank housing policy first.

Candidates compete with supply expansion, eased redevelopment rules and support for young and newlywed households—though funding and feasibility need scrutiny.

Experts say the sharper the felt 'asset gap,' the more votes swing, and that concrete delivery plans, not slogans, will decide trust.
