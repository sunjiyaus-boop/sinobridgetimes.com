기록적 폭염에 시달리는 일본 도쿄에서 더위를 이기기 위해 ‘반바지 출근’이 권장되자, 일부 여성들이 “불공평하다”며 반발하고 나섰다. 복장 규범과 젠더 형평 논의로 번졌다.
마약란 기자 · 2026.07.23

롄허보에 따르면 폭염 대응책으로 반바지 출근이 제시되자, 여성들 사이에서 남성과 다른 사회적 시선과 부담을 지적하는 목소리가 나왔다.

By Ma Yak-ran · 2026.07.23

Per United Daily News, after shorts-to-work was floated as a heat measure, some women pointed to social gaze and burdens different from men's.

광고 문의 · 300×250

Women cited conditions unlike men's—the gaze that comes with wearing shorts and social pressure over so-called 'leg hair.'

Dress freedom adds convenience, but equity issues surface when social standards apply differently by gender.

As climate change worsens summer heat, workplace dress norms are shifting, but critics say the change does not grant everyone the same freedom.

Even a small dress norm carries a society's customs and sense of equity; a discussion weighing both convenience and fairness is needed.
