'Only the first shot': is Silicon Valley's layoff wave the opening of an AI shakeout?

Signs of Silicon Valley's layoff wave appeared early, and a departed Meta AI figure says the cuts are 'only the first shot,' the United Daily News reported.
By Hyeong Deok-chang · 2026.07.07

Speaking about the circumstances of his exit, he judged Big Tech's workforce restructuring to be the opening phase of an AI-centered reorganization, per UDN.

광고 문의 · 300×250

The majors are pouring money into AI infrastructure while squeezing existing organizations—a dual movement of investing and cutting that is becoming the industry norm.

The gap between jobs AI creates and replaces, and the retraining question, are rising on labor-policy agendas worldwide.

For IT workers and students from Korea and Taiwan, and Chinese youth eyeing U.S. jobs, the shifting hiring winds are a direct variable.

Second-half hiring plans and earnings reports are the next gauges of how fast the shakeout runs.
