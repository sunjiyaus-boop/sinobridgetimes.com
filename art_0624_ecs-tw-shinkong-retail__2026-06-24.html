Shadow and Light of Retail Restructuring: Taiwan's Shifting Store Landscape

Prompted by closure talk at an old Tainan department store, structural change in Taiwan's retail is back in focus, as the industry seeks a path between online shift and experiential stores.
By Yang Nyeom-ja · 2026.06.24

Closure speculation at a 30-year-old Tainan department store has spotlighted structural change across Taiwan's retail, udn and others reported, as traditional department stores reshape in earnest.

광고 문의 · 300×250

E-commerce growth is redefining the role of physical stores, demanding a shift from mere selling to spaces offering experience and content.

Department stores and hypermarkets seek paths via mixed cultural spaces, experiential stores and online-offline integration, with location and content key to survival.

Retail change also affects jobs and local commerce; a single closure can be not an end but a turning point toward new forms of commercial space.

This paper conveys Taiwan's retail restructuring fact-first, noting the direction of consumption trends.
