據경향신문，濟州週末戲水事故致1死、3人遭水母螫傷。夏季離岸流、水深驟變與海洋生物接觸等多重風險交疊。

One Dead in Weekend Water Accident on Jeju; Jellyfish Stings Follow

One person died during weekend water play on Jeju, and jellyfish stings followed. Ahead of the peak season, caution over water safety is urged.
By Ma Yak-ran · 2026.07.23

One person died and three were stung by jellyfish in weekend water accidents on Jeju, Kyunghyang reported. Summer brings overlapping risks—rip currents, sudden depth changes and contact with marine life.

광고 문의 · 300×250

For water safety, follow lifeguards in designated areas, avoid entering the water after drinking, and keep children with a guardian at all times. If stung by a jellyfish, do not rub it; seek a lifeguard's help at once.

Authorities are strengthening beach safety management and hazard alerts. Checking weather and ocean information in advance and avoiding forced entry are the basics of prevention.
