By Son Gi-yo · 2026.06.19

United Daily News and the Taipei Times reported the Cabinet passed a drone budget bill of about US$6.6bn, aiming to strengthen posture by acquiring uncrewed forces at scale.

Drones and USVs are cost-effective and low-risk to personnel, seen as central to asymmetric defense. Taiwan aims to lift reconnaissance, surveillance and contingency response with this buy.
