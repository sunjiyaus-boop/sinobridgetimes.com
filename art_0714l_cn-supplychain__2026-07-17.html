서방 기관이 ‘중국 공급망 의존 탈피’에 드는 비용을 23조 6천억 달러로 추산했다는 보도가 나왔다. 대체 공급망 구축의 현실적 부담을 보여주는 수치다.
전금문 기자 · 2026.07.17

환구망에 따르면 서방 기관의 시산 결과 중국 공급망에서 벗어나는 데 드는 총비용이 23조 6천억 달러에 이르는 것으로 계산됐다.

『擺脫中國供應鏈』的帳單…西方機構估算23.6兆美元

有報導稱，西方機構估算『擺脫對中國供應鏈依賴』的成本高達23.6兆美元。這一數字凸顯重建替代供應鏈的現實負擔。
本報記者 錢錦汶 · 2026.07.17

據環球網報導，西方機構試算結果顯示，脫離中國供應鏈的總成本達23.6兆美元。

광고 문의 · 300×250

據悉，該數字涵蓋生產基地轉移、替代採購來源確保、基礎設施重建，以及過程中的效率損失。

供應鏈重組之爭，歸根結柢是成本與安全之間的權衡。此次試算被視為以數字呈現天平上『成本』一端的分量。

The bill for 'de-risking' from China: $23.6 trillion, Western estimate says

A Western institution has put the cost of ending reliance on Chinese supply chains at $23.6 trillion, a figure underscoring the practical burden of building alternatives.
By Jeon Geum-mun · 2026.07.17

The estimate tallies the total cost of moving away from China-centered supply chains at $23.6 trillion, Huanqiu reported.

광고 문의 · 300×250

The figure reportedly covers relocating production, securing alternative suppliers, rebuilding infrastructure and the efficiency losses along the way.

China's industrial ecosystem runs end to end from materials processing to components and final assembly, making wholesale substitution difficult even where individual items can be replaced, analysts say.

In practice, many multinationals have settled on a 'China plus one' hedge — diversifying risk while keeping Chinese production.

The supply-chain debate is ultimately a scale weighing cost against security; this estimate puts a number on the cost side.
