By Yang Nyeom-ja · 2026.06.29

The government named pension reform as the most contested task in social debate, a policy briefing said. Interests diverge over the contribution rate, the income replacement rate and the age at which benefits begin.

As aging deepens fears of fund depletion, there is consensus on the urgency of reform, but generations and classes differ sharply over how to share the burden.

The government said it would seek a phased adjustment through labor-management-government and expert consultation. The breadth of consensus will determine the reform's durability.
