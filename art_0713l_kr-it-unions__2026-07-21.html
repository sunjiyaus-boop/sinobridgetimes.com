경향신문에 따르면 삼성·현대차·신세계 그룹의 IT 서비스 계열사들에서 최근 잇따라 노조가 만들어지고 있다.

IT 업계는 전통적으로 노조 조직률이 낮았지만, AI 자동화와 구조조정 우려가 커지면서 분위기가 달라졌다는 분석이다.

Union wave reaches IT arms of Samsung, Hyundai and Shinsegae amid job anxiety

Labor unions are springing up at IT affiliates of Korea's biggest groups, the Kyunghyang Shinmun reported, with AI-era job insecurity cited as the driver.
By Jang Ip-gun · 2026.07.21

IT service units of Samsung, Hyundai Motor and Shinsegae groups have recently seen a string of new unions, according to the paper.

광고 문의 · 300×250

The tech sector's unionization rate has been low, but automation and restructuring fears are changing the mood, analysts say.

Young, developer-heavy workforces are leading the drives, demanding transparent treatment and job security.

Union growth at these affiliates could reshape labor relations and wage bargaining across the conglomerates.

Experts say preemptive safety nets — retraining and redeployment — are key to heading off conflict in the technology transition.
