Heat wave grips Europe as large wildfires burn across several countries

Sustained high temperatures have triggered large wildfires across multiple European countries, Huanqiu reported—heat-driven fires are becoming a fixture of European summers.
By Wang Suk-yeong · 2026.07.08

Hot, dry weather centered on the Iberian Peninsula and the Mediterranean coast has sparked successive fires, forcing evacuations and road closures in some areas.

광고 문의 · 300×250

Fire authorities are mobilizing aerial assets but struggle against strong winds and parched vegetation.

Climatologists note that stronger, more frequent heat waves are lengthening southern Europe's fire season, and the overlap with peak tourism deepens economic losses.

Talk of EU-level joint firefighting assets and stronger early-warning systems is gaining momentum again.

Heat and fires also hit harvests and power demand, potentially feeding European-driven inflation this summer.
