「熱壓力最危險時段非午後」…嘉義最嚴峻

'The Most Dangerous Heat-Stress Hour Isn't the Afternoon'—Chiayi Worst-Hit

A Taiwanese study finds the most dangerous time for 'heat stress' in extreme heat is not midday. Among all regions, Chiayi was found to be the most severe.
By Kang Pae-san · 2026.06.30

Per the United Daily News, applying a 'heat-stress' index combining temperature, humidity and radiant heat, the study found the riskiest window may not be just after noon, and that Chiayi was the harshest by region.

Heat stress reflects the heat the body actually feels better than temperature alone, making it a more useful index for preventing heat illness.
