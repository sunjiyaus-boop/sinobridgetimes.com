越南開罰社群假訊息　本月起執行

越南自本月1日起，對在社群媒體散布假訊息之用戶開罰。此舉強化網路資訊管控，亦引發表達自由萎縮之憂。
本報記者 孫承宗 · 2026.07.12

據外電，新規定對發布、分享不實資訊行為，個人亦可處罰款。

政府以防止詐騙與謠言致社會混亂為由；人權團體則憂心標準模糊，恐被用於壓制批評聲音。

Vietnam begins fining social media users for fake news

From July 1, Vietnam began fining social media users who spread false information — a tightening of online control that also raises free-expression concerns.
By Son Seung-jong · 2026.07.12

Reports say the new rules allow fines on individuals for posting or sharing falsehoods.

광고 문의 · 300×250

Vietnam has among the world's highest social media usage, with Facebook, TikTok and local platforms shaping opinion.

The government cites prevention of fraud and rumor-driven disorder; rights groups worry vague standards could target criticism.

Southeast Asian countries have rolled out fake-news laws in succession, with ongoing debate over their use.

Foreigners doing business or traveling in Vietnam should know the local rules on online posts.
