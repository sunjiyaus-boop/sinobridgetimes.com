'Meta shock' hammers Samsung Electronics and SK Hynix, rattling chip stocks

Shares of Samsung Electronics and SK Hynix tumbled in the wake of Meta's announcement, shaking chip stocks that had ridden AI-infrastructure hopes and leading the KOSPI lower.
By Im Suk-bun · 2026.07.20

According to Chosun Ilbo, the Meta news fed anxiety over memory demand, drawing heavy selling in Korea's chip heavyweights.

광고 문의 · 300×250

If talk of slower AI data-center spending gains ground, forecasts for high-bandwidth memory could be rebased—an anxiety visible in the sell-off.

Semiconductors anchor Korean exports and KOSPI capitalization, so a chip rout ripples into the index and the won.

Some argue the structural growth of AI demand remains intact and the reaction is overdone.

The contrast with TSMC's target-price rally makes the Korea-Taiwan temperature gap the thing to watch.
