Six Mystery 'Space Balls' Found in Australia May Hold Toxic Rocket Fuel

Six unidentified metal spheres found across Australia have triggered an investigation. Believed to be space-launch debris, they may still contain highly toxic rocket fuel.
By Son Gi-yo · 2026.07.15

According to Liberty Times, the spheres found in remote areas are thought to be fuel-tank components from launch vehicles.

광고 문의 · 300×250

Experts warn hydrazine-class toxic fuel could remain in the debris and urge the public not to approach or touch them.

With launches surging, falling orbital debris is more common, energizing international debate over responsibility for cleanup.

Residents in the areas have been told to report any similar objects immediately.
