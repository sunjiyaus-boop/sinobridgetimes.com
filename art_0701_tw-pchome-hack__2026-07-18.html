By Bang Mun-yeon · 2026.07.18

PChome was hacked and the company moved to gauge the damage and tighten security, urging users to change passwords and take care, United Daily reported.

광고 문의 · 300×250

E-commerce platforms concentrate payments and personal data, making them easy cyber targets; once breached, many users are exposed at once.

Authorities and the company are investigating the intrusion path and confirming whether and how much data leaked; users should watch for suspicious payment or login alerts.

Experts stress that pre-emptive security investment and prompt incident disclosure are key to limiting harm.
