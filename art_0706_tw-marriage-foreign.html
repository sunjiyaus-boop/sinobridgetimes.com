台灣近五分之一婚姻為跨國聯姻 外籍配偶占18.5%

台灣與外籍配偶的婚姻占整體18.5%，接近每五對就有一對，是台灣社會多元文化轉型的指標。
本報記者 周裕民 · 2026.07.06

據《自由時報》系《台北時報》報導，最新統計顯示與外國籍配偶的婚姻占整體婚姻的18.5%。

광고 문의 · 300×250

配偶來源地以東南亞、中國大陸與港澳占較大比重。

隨著跨國婚姻家庭增加，雙語教育、居留與國籍制度、社會融合政策的需求也同步擴大。

如同韓國華僑社會，台灣的多元文化家庭也因語言與認同資產受到重視，兩地制度比較對華人家庭具參考價值。

Nearly One in Five Taiwan Marriages Involves a Foreign Spouse at 18.5%

Foreign spouses account for 18.5% of marriages in Taiwan — nearly one in five — a marker of the island's multicultural shift.
By Ju Yu-min · 2026.07.06

According to the Taipei Times, affiliated with Liberty Times, the latest statistics put marriages with foreign nationals at 18.5% of the total.

광고 문의 · 300×250

Spouses largely come from Southeast Asia, mainland China, and Hong Kong and Macau.

The rise of cross-border families is expanding demand for bilingual education, residency and nationality systems, and integration policy.

Like Korea's overseas-Chinese community, Taiwan's multicultural families are valued for language and identity assets; comparing the two systems offers useful reference for Chinese families.
