Taiwan police suspected of leaking internal information; some detained, some bailed

In Taiwan, police officers are under prosecutorial investigation over suspicions of leaking investigative information to a fraud ring. Some were detained, others released on bail.
By Son Gi-yo · 2026.07.14

Prosecutors investigated several officers over leaks and taking money, ordering detention for some and bail for others, the Liberty Times and United Daily reported.

광고 문의 · 300×250

When insiders in an investigative agency leak information, it can hamper pursuit of criminal organizations and harm citizens; 'internal misconduct' shakes the foundation of trust.

Authorities are confirming the leak routes and the scope of involvement and widening the probe; specific charges and dispositions will be disclosed in due course.
