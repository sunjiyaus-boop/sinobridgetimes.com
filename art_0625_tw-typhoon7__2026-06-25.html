여름철 태풍과 집중호우는 침수·산사태·교통 차질 등 복합 재해로 이어질 수 있다. 기상 당국은 진로와 강수량에 따라 지역별 경보를 갱신하며, 저지대 침수와 하천 범람에 대한 사전 대비를 강조한다.

농업·관광·물류는 기상에 민감하다. 수확기 농작물 피해나 교통 지연은 시차를 두고 물가와 공급에 영향을 줄 수 있어, 재해 대비는 곧 생활·경제의 안정과 연결된다.

華橋時報는 재해 보도에서 ‘공포’가 아니라 ‘준비’를 전한다. 정확한 예보 확인, 저지대·하천 접근 자제, 비상 연락망 점검이 피해를 줄이는 기본이다.

夏季颱風與強降雨，恐釀淹水、山崩、交通受阻等複合災害。氣象當局依路徑與雨量更新各地警報，並強調對低窪淹水與河川氾濫之事前防備。

農業、觀光、物流對天候敏感。收穫期作物受損或交通延誤，恐經時間遞延影響物價與供給，故防災即連結生活與經濟之安定。

As Typhoon No. 7 moves north, Taiwan's weather bureau issued heavy-rain advisories for the south and urged preparation, Liberty Times reported.
By Yu Seon-gyeong · 2026.06.25

With Typhoon No. 7 moving north, Taiwan's Central Weather Administration said heavy rain could fall in southern areas including Kaohsiung, Chiayi, Pingtung and Tainan, issuing heavy-rain advisories and urging caution, Taiwan's Liberty Times reported on June 24.

광고 문의 · 300×250

Summer typhoons and concentrated rain can lead to compound disasters—flooding, landslides, transport disruption. Weather authorities update regional warnings by track and rainfall, stressing advance preparation against low-lying flooding and river overflow.

Agriculture, tourism and logistics are sensitive to weather. Damage to harvest-season crops or transport delays can affect prices and supply with a lag, so disaster preparedness is tied to the stability of life and the economy.

Sinophone Bridge Times conveys 'preparation,' not 'fear,' in disaster coverage. Checking accurate forecasts, avoiding low-lying areas and rivers, and reviewing emergency contacts are the basics of reducing harm.
