도를 넘은 '팬덤'의 그늘

By Jo Gye-hyeon · 2026.06.21

An attack in which a cheerleader for a Taiwanese pro-baseball team was wounded in the neck with a weapon shocked society. Police arrested a suspect, but the exact motive has yet to be established.

광고 문의 · 300×250

The arrested suspect is reported to be the victim's so-called 'super fan.' He is said to be refusing to state his motive during the investigation.

The shadow of 'fandom' gone too far

The case shows what danger the twisted obsession of some zealous fans toward public figures or entertainers can lead to. When excessive devotion turns into violence, the harm is hard to undo.

Experts point to the need for safety protections for those in publicly visible professions and for social attention that catches warning signs early. Discussion of the healthy boundaries of fan culture is also called for.

Hwagyo Times holds the focus should be on the victim's recovery and preventing recurrence rather than on the sensational side of the case. Support for public figures becomes a healthy culture only when it keeps the line of respect and distance.
