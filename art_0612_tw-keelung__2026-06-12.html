Chlorosulfonic Acid Leak Causes Scare at Keelung Port

A chlorosulfonic acid leak produced fumes at Keelung Port; the operator blamed an aged gasket exposed to rain.
By Bang Mun-yeon · 2026.06.12

Per United Daily News, China Chemical said the substance was not a semiconductor material and fumes likely arose when rain hit a worn gasket.

광고 문의 · 300×250

Firefighters controlled the scene; no casualties were reported.

Chlorosulfonic acid emits irritating fumes on contact with water.

The incident renewed calls to inspect aging chemical-handling facilities at ports.

Findings and repair plans will be announced after investigation.
