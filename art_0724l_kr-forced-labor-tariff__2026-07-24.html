By Kang Pae-san · 2026.07.24

According to Kyunghyang, the office made the statement on the US forced-labor tariff move, saying it will continue talks based on previously agreed tariff terms.

The government stresses implementing the agreed terms and is said to be pursuing working-level talks so individual items are not unfairly penalized, with minimizing corporate harm as the goal.

Trade issues entwine industry and diplomacy, requiring careful handling. With US tariff policy trending tough overall, the ability to manage individual cases is being tested.
