연금과 함께 노동 개혁이 상반기 핵심 의제로 부상했다. 근로시간 유연화와 노동시장 이중구조 개선이 쟁점으로 거론된다.
유종한 기자 · 2026.06.29

Labor reform also moves ahead; talk of reworking work hours and the dual structure

Alongside pensions, labor reform has emerged as a key first-half agenda. Flexible work hours and improving the labor market's dual structure are cited as issues.
By Yu Jong-han · 2026.06.29

The government said it would pursue labor reform as one pillar of the six reforms, a policy briefing said. Closing the gap between large-firm regular workers and small-firm, non-regular workers is named as a task.

광고 문의 · 300×250

The heart of the debate is how to reconcile flexibility in work-hour rules with protecting the right to health. Labor and management see it very differently.

The government said it would pursue phased reform through social dialogue, but reconciling interests is likely to take time.

Sinophone Bridge Times conveys the labor agenda through the real effects of institutional change, not a tilt toward labor or management.
