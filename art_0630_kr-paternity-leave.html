'Dad Prosecutors' Take Parental Leave Too—Doubling in a Year

Male prosecutors' use of parental leave has doubled in a year, a sign that work-family balance is spreading even in an organization seen as conservative.
By Wang Suk-yeong · 2026.06.30

Per the Chosun Ilbo's 'Seocho-dong 25 Hours,' the number of male prosecutors taking parental leave roughly doubled over the past year, with analysts noting the perception that 'you can't use it even if it exists' is fading.

광고 문의 · 300×250

The spread of paternal leave is part of a trend toward sharing childbearing and childcare burdens within the family; similar shifts are seen across the public and private sectors.

Will it stick as culture?

The key is not a one-off rise but establishing an atmosphere where leave can be taken, making follow-up safeguards against disadvantages in evaluation or promotion after return important.

Sinophone Bridge Times reports the shift in work-family balance in its broader social context.
