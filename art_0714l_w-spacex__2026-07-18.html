SpaceX shares fall for second day, nearing $135 IPO price

SpaceX stock slid for a second straight day, closing in on its $135 IPO price as the post-listing froth meets a reality check.
By Kang Pae-san · 2026.07.18

Shares fell again, approaching the offering price of $135, the Liberty Times reported.

광고 문의 · 300×250

The space bellwether soared past its IPO price on early enthusiasm, but valuation stretched beyond growth expectations has drawn repeated warnings.

Starlink profitability, the Starship development timeline and reliance on government contracts are the key variables for investors.

Middle East risk aversion, which is dragging down richly valued growth stocks across the board, deepened the fall.

Whether the IPO price holds is seen as a symbolic gauge of sentiment toward the space sector at large.
