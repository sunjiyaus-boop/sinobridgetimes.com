PHEV는 평소 짧은 거리는 전기로 주행하고, 장거리나 충전이 어려울 때는 휘발유 엔진을 함께 쓰는 방식이다. 충전 인프라가 충분치 않은 환경에서 전동화의 이점을 누리려는 소비자에게 현실적 대안으로 꼽힌다.

전동화의 과도기적 선택

PHEV는 순수 전기차로 가는 과도기의 현실적 선택지로 평가된다. 일상에서는 전기차처럼 쓰고, 충전 걱정 없이 장거리도 소화할 수 있다는 점이 강점이다.

다만 배터리와 엔진을 함께 탑재해 차량 가격이 높고, 충전을 게을리하면 그 이점이 줄어든다는 한계도 있다. 자신의 주행 패턴에 맞는 선택이 중요하다는 조언이 따른다.

電動化的過渡選擇

PHEV被評為邁向純電動車過渡期的務實選項。日常如電動車般使用、又能無充電之憂消化長途，為其強項。

惟因同時搭載電池與引擎、車價偏高，且疏於充電其益處便減，亦有此限制。有建議指出，依自身行駛型態選擇至為重要。

Is a plug-in hybrid worth buying? A Toyota RAV4 PHEV test drive

Interest is high in plug-in hybrids (PHEVs), which combine the strengths of EVs and combustion engines. A test drive of the Toyota RAV4 PHEV examines its practicality and limits together.
By Hyeong Deok-chang · 2026.06.21

Consumer interest continues in plug-in hybrids, which can use both charging and refueling. A test drive of the representative Toyota RAV4 PHEV considers the pros and cons of this approach.

광고 문의 · 300×250

A PHEV runs on electricity for short everyday trips and uses the gasoline engine for long distances or when charging is hard. It is cited as a realistic alternative for consumers who want the benefits of electrification where charging infrastructure is insufficient.

A transitional choice in electrification

A PHEV is rated as a realistic option for the transition toward pure EVs. Its strengths are using it like an EV in daily life while handling long distances without range anxiety.

But it also has limits: carrying both battery and engine makes the vehicle pricier, and the benefit shrinks if one neglects charging. The advice follows that a choice matching one's own driving pattern matters.

Hwagyo Times notes that buying a car is a decision that must weigh one's lifestyle and environment together. Rather than which powertrain is better, the wisest choice is the one that suits oneself.
