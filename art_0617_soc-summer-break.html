Countdown to Summer Break's 'School-Gate Closing'; Plan Ahead

As Taiwan's school summer break nears, the 'school-gate closing' has entered a countdown. Guidance keeps coming for families that have not yet set plans.
By Yeo Nyeon-jae · 2026.06.17

With the summer break imminent at Taiwan's schools, a long 'school-gate closing' is set to begin, the Liberty Times reported. Many families are pondering how to arrange their children's schedules and care during the break.

광고 문의 · 300×250

A long break is a chance for rest and growth for children, but poses a care gap for dual-income families. Options such as camps, after-care classes and experiential programs are discussed.

For break plans, a balance of study, rest and experience matters. Realistic plans suited to a child's interests and the family's circumstances are recommended over overloaded schedules.

The Bridge Times conveys education and daily-life facts. Break-time care and children's education are universal tasks faced by every family, including Chinese households in Korea.
