Taiwan's drone bills advance to committee review

Drone-related bills in Taiwan's legislature have moved to committee review, the Taipei Times reported — institutionalizing both industry promotion and defense against unmanned aircraft.
By Jeon Geum-mun · 2026.07.16

The bills reportedly cover industry support, airspace management and powers to counter illegal drones.

광고 문의 · 300×250

Since the war in Ukraine, Taiwan has increasingly viewed drones as core asymmetric capability.

On the industrial side, localizing components and opening export markets are the stated tasks.

Partisan sparring over regulatory intensity and budgets is expected during review.

Drone supply chains offer sizable room for Korea-Taiwan corporate cooperation, watched alongside cross-strait dynamics.
