미국 각지에서는 이민 당국의 차량 검문과 추격 과정에서 사망 사고가 이어져 논란이 커져 왔다. 인권 단체들은 과잉 단속이 비극을 낳고 있다고 비판한다.

단속의 정당성과 인명 피해 사이의 긴장은 미국 이민 정치의 오래된 단층선이다. 중간선거를 앞두고 이 갈등은 더 첨예해질 전망이다.

Trump says immigrant vehicle checks “must continue” despite string of deaths

Even as enforcement-related fatalities mount, the president holds to hardline deportations — and immigrant communities brace.
By Yu Seon-gyeong · 2026.07.21

President Donald Trump said vehicle checks in immigration enforcement must continue, the Kyunghyang Shinmun reported — reaffirming a hard line even as deaths linked to the crackdowns mount.

광고 문의 · 300×250

Fatal incidents during stops and pursuits by immigration authorities have stirred controversy across the U.S., with rights groups charging that aggressive enforcement is producing tragedies.

The administration touts deportation numbers as a signature achievement and has widened checkpoints and raids. With enforcement reaching workplaces and school runs, daily life in immigrant neighborhoods is shrinking.

Immigrant communities, including Asian Americans, worry that even lawful residents are being swept into checks. Bar associations are stepping up guidance on carrying documents and knowing one's rights.

The tension between enforcement and loss of life is an old fault line in American immigration politics — one set to sharpen ahead of the midterms.
