파키스탄이 신장 면화 종자 도입을 추진하는 등 중국과의 면화 협력을 확대할 계획이라고 環球網이 전했다.
방문연 기자 · 2026.07.08

파키스탄 측 인사는 “양국 면화 협력의 공간이 매우 크다”며 우량 종자와 재배 기술 도입에 대한 기대를 밝혔다.

'Vast room for Pakistan-China cotton cooperation': Pakistan moves to adopt Xinjiang cotton seeds

Pakistan plans to introduce Xinjiang cotton seeds as it expands cotton cooperation with China, Huanqiu reported.
By Bang Mun-yeon · 2026.07.08

A Pakistani official said the two countries have 'vast room' for cotton cooperation, hoping to adopt quality seeds and cultivation techniques.

광고 문의 · 300×250

Pakistan is a major cotton producer but has seen yields stagnate under climate change and pests.

Xinjiang seed varieties and water-saving techniques could strengthen the raw-material base of Pakistan's textile industry, observers say.

Analysts link the move to the agricultural agenda of CPEC's second phase.

With Chinese diaspora capital and buyers deeply embedded in South Asia's textile chain, the cooperation's ripple effects bear watching.
