陽明交大前專員偽印詐款未遂 離職刪除大量師生資料判刑

陽明交通大學前專員偽造印章詐領款項未遂，並於離職時大量刪除師生資料，遭判刑，聯合報5日報導。
本報記者 柳善卿 · 2026.07.17

判決指出，他在電資專班承辦行政業務期間，偽造文件印章申領經費，被內部核銷程序識破。

광고 문의 · 300×250

離職過程中，他登入系上共用系統，大量刪除教學與研究檔案、癱瘓業務，成為量刑重點。

Former NYCU Staffer Convicted over Forged Seals and Mass Deletion of Faculty-Student Data

A former specialist at Taiwan's National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University was convicted after forging seals in a failed attempt to claim funds and mass-deleting faculty and student files upon leaving, the United Daily News reported Sunday.
By Yu Seon-gyeong · 2026.07.17

The ruling found he forged document seals to claim expenses while handling administration for an electronics program, but internal verification caught the attempt.

광고 문의 · 300×250

Heavier weight fell on his deletion of teaching and research files from shared systems during his departure, paralyzing operations.

The court convicted him of document forgery and destruction of electronic records, imposing a prison term; the university is restoring data and tightening access controls.

The case shows insider data destruction can be as damaging as external hacking, prompting Taiwanese universities to review account revocation and backup procedures.

Information-security management at educational institutions is a shared challenge for Korean universities too, where lax handling of departed employees' accounts draws repeated criticism.
