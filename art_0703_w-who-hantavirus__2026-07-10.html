WHO declares cruise-ship hantavirus outbreak over as last contact tests negative

The World Health Organization declared an end to the hantavirus outbreak aboard a cruise ship after the final contact tested negative, the Liberty Times reported.
By Jo Gye-hyeon · 2026.07.10

No further infections emerged during the surveillance period, clearing the way for the all-clear.

광고 문의 · 300×250

Hantavirus spreads mainly via rodent droppings and rarely between people; an outbreak in a sealed shipboard environment had put health authorities on alert.

Cruise operators are keeping tightened pre-boarding screening and onboard sanitation in place.

Travelers eyeing summer cruises should check operators' health notices and destination disease advisories.
