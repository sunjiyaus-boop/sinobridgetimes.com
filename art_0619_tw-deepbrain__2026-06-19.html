자유시보에 따르면 대만 담수이 마카이병원이 정밀 신경조절 방식의 심부뇌자극술(DBS)을 통해 파킨슨병 치료에 새로운 진전을 이뤘다. 뇌의 특정 부위에 미세 전기 자극을 주어 운동 증상을 조절하는 치료법이다.

심부뇌자극술은 약물로 조절이 어려운 진행성 파킨슨병 환자에게 선택지가 될 수 있다. 떨림·경직·운동 완만 같은 증상을 완화해 일상생활의 질을 높이는 데 도움을 준다.

Tamsui Mackay Hospital Opens a New Chapter in Parkinson's Care With Deep Brain Stimulation

Taiwan's Tamsui Mackay Hospital opened a new chapter in Parkinson's treatment with deep brain stimulation (DBS), a precise neuromodulation technique that helps ease tremor and rigidity.
By Jeon Geum-mun · 2026.06.19

Per the Liberty Times, Tamsui Mackay Hospital in Taiwan made new progress in Parkinson's treatment using deep brain stimulation (DBS), a precise neuromodulation method that delivers fine electrical stimulation to specific brain regions to manage motor symptoms.

광고 문의 · 300×250

DBS can be an option for advanced Parkinson's patients hard to control with medication, easing tremor, rigidity and slowed movement to improve quality of daily life.

It is not for every patient, however; indications, timing and risks must be decided in full consultation with specialists. Pre- and post-procedure assessment and careful tuning determine the outcome.

This paper conveys medical and health information accurately. As Parkinson's is a major disease in an aging society, advances in treatment are meaningful news for Chinese families too.
