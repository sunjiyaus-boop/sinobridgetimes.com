왕숙영 기자 · 2026.07.24

Prosecutors Book Gwangsan Police Chief Over Botched Investigation, Raid His Office

Prosecutors booked the chief of Gwangju's Gwangsan Police Station over allegations of a botched investigation in the so-called Jang Yun-gi case and raided his office, the Kyunghyang Shinmun reported — putting a front-line police investigation commander directly under scrutiny.
By Wang Suk-yeong · 2026.07.24

Investigators reportedly secured case records and internal reports from the chief's office and related units to check whether necessary steps were skipped in the initial probe.

광고 문의 · 300×250

The core question is whether lapses at the police stage delayed the case or worsened the harm.

Booking and raiding a sitting station chief carries delicate implications for prosecution-police relations, and calls are rising within the police for a review of investigative practices.

Prosecutors plan to summon those involved after analyzing the seized materials.

Civil society is watching whether mutual checks between agencies lead to real accountability.
